The SimSearch algorithm has been developed for the GenData project as a collaboration between University of Bologna and Politecnico di Milano. For more information about the SimSearch algorithm, please read Pattern Similarity Search in Genomic Sequences (Montanari et al. 2016 IEEE Transactions on Knowledge and Data Engineering).

The SimSearch App for IGB, based on the SimSearch algorithm, is developped by @arnaudceol from the Istituto Italiano di Tecnologia.

SimSearch is now part of the DATA-DRIVEN GENOMIC COMPUTING (GeCo) project.
